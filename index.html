Feiran Wang

Hi, I'm Feiran, a PhD student in computer vision at Illinois Tech since 2024, under the supervision of Professor Yan Yan. I'm visiting the University of Illinois Chicago in Fall 2025. I earned my Master's degree in Engineering from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I received research training under Professor David Forsyth. Before that, I completed my Bachelor's degree at Shanghai University, guided by Professor Xiaoqiang Li. In Fall 2021, I studied at the University of Toronto as a visiting student.

My research interests focus on integrating the physical world into the digital domain, with a particular emphasis on 3D reconstruction, image generation. Additionally, I am interested in advancing research in medical imaging.
