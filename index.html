Feiran Wang

Hi, I'm Feiran, a second-year PhD student in computer vision at Illinois Tech, advised by Professor Yan Yan. I'm currently visiting UIC. I hold a M.S. from UIUC, where I worked with Professor David Forsyth, and a B.S. from Shanghai University, advised by Professor Xiaoqiang Li. I was an exchange student at the University of Toronto.

My research focuses on integrating the physical world into the digital domain and creating real-world impact, with particular emphasis on 3D reconstruction, vision foundation models, medical imaging, and generative AI.
